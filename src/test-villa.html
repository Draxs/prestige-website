Ground Floor

The ground floor offers an exceptionally spacious living area featuring a working fireplace, ample sofa seating, coffee tables and a small library with book collection. For your entertainment there is a Flat screen internet TV, DVD player and wireless internet connection. Separated by an archway is a generously sized kitchen, fully equipped with an electric oven and hob, fridge/freezer, microwave, coffee machine, toaster dishwasher and dining table seating 6. The utility room housing the washing machine is joined to the kitchen by a connecting door. Adjacent to the living room is a dining area with patio doors leading to a swimming pool terrace. The first of the bedrooms is located on the ground floor. A couple of steps lead you to the spacious double bedroom suite with a wardrobe, large and modern en-suite bathroom, bay windows and patio doors with direct access to the terrace. A small corridor leading to a shower room and a separate entrance from the parking area complete this floor.

First Floor

Stairs take you to the first floor’s corridor and remaining bedrooms (there is a child safety gate at the top of the stairs). The spacious master double bedroom features a walk-in wardrobe, grand bathroom with Jacuzzi bath tub, ‘his and her’ sinks, bay windows and patio doors with access to balcony boasting lovely views over the swimming pool area, sea and green surroundings. The remaining three bedrooms on this level comprise of two twin bedrooms and a double bedroom,sharing a family bathroom with bathtub, toilet, and ‘his and her’ sinks. One of the twin bedrooms offers patio doors leading to a balcony. A practical kitchenette equipped with a small fridge and sink is located along the hallway, very useful to have your cold drinks handy. The villa has air-conditioning throughout. Baby cot and high chair can be provided on request.

Outside area

Outside the 12m x 6m private heated swimming pool (see rental rates) with walk-in steps is surrounded by a substantial sunbathing terrace equipped with sunbeds, umbrellas and a poolside shower. There is purpose built barbecue area with built in barbecue and additional sink unit as well as a shaded seating area, perfect for al fresco dining. Immediately surrounding the villa are carefully manicured green areas abundant with trees, herbs and shrubbery. With its lovely private location; this is a wonderful venue for hosting small family celebrations, retreats or weddings etc (please contact us for details). The villa offers a shaded parking space for up to 2 vehicles with additional parking space available along the driveway.

Restaurant

Families will appreciate a nice snack bar/restaurant located 420 m from the villa, offering a playground and grass area as well as nice seating providing beautiful views of the sea and amazing sea caves formations. There is also a path at the sea front leading to another restaurant (operating in summer months) nearby – 1.2 km. For a taste of the more traditional, the Church at St George overlooks the modest fishing harbour from where you can enjoy some of the islands most memorable sunset views and enjoy a traditional Cypriot meze meal at one of the small selection of family run tavernas.

Coast/Beach

The Sea Caves area is situated in a peaceful location between the popular resort of Coral Bay and the tiny fishing hamlet of St Georges. The coastline in this area is full of natural rock caves making it ideal snorkeling territory, however if you prefer an organised beach then the family oriented resort of Corallia & Coral Bay is less than 10 minutes drive away. Coral Bay is the well known beach resort for the Paphos district and has become a lively resort with plenty to offer in terms of amenities and activities. The centre of the resort features a wide selection of restaurants offering both Cypriot and International Cuisines, cafes, bars, shops, a choice of supermarkets, several banks, a chemist and a laundry. The resort boasts two sandy beaches, the closest one to the villa being Corallia Bay, the quieter of the two. A little further along the coast is the, the popular and busy Coral Bay Beach, a sandy horseshoe shaped bay honoured with a Blue Flag of the European Union. Sunbeds and umbrellas are available for hire, watersports are on offer and there are two beach cafés providing refreshments.

Activities

Mini golf, watersports and go-karting are all activities available in the resort of Coral Bay and if you extend your exploring to encompass the town of Paphos, the list of available activities expands to include a bowling alley, waterpark, several Heritage sites, attractive harbour area and modern shopping facilities. For the golf enthusiasts, there are 4 golf courses; Tsada Golf Course, Secret Valley Golf Course, Aphrodite Hills Golf Course and Elea Golf Course all within approximately a 30 minute drive.

Attraction

The Paphos Zoo and the Horse Riding Stables which cater for all ages are both within the Sea Caves area and only a few minutes drive away. For nature lovers the start of the protected Akamas Peninsula is just to the north of the village. Paphos Town approximately 13 kilometres away, was once the capital of the island is now included in the official UNESCO list of cultural and natural treasures of the world’s heritage. The remains of villas, palaces, theatres, fortresses and tombs mean that the town is of exceptional architectural and historic value. The well preserved mosaics and the Tombs of the Kings are probably the biggest cultural draw with further archeological discoveries continually being made. Ano Pafos on the cliff is the main administrative centre and residential district while Kato Pafos, by the sea, is the main tourist area built around the harbour and medieval port.
